Dearest, My Brother.
Sol, before my era is totally over in your life story, I always wanted to say that I never want you to be more than who you are right now. You've been through a thousand blue days and nights and I'm so proud that I'm one of the witnesses who saw how hard you survived.

I still clearly remember long ago, when I was 14, we talked about many things (literally everything) late into the night. I even once was a part of your group of friends. We once had a birthday party on the football field on a rainy afternoon. We sat by the sea and talked about me going to high school. You drove me to "someone's house" just to deliver his birthday present (it's gonna be a useless memory if I didn't go with you). We fought a few times and ignore each other but still handle it together, becoming best friends and brothers again.

Now we grew up. Unconsciously, we force ourselves to handle everything on our own. I'm not your safe place anymore and I feel sorry about that. I don't want to ask you to go back here with me or force you to put me back into your life story anymore. All I hope is, please let someone hold and love you right. Yes, both of us agree that the safest person we can ever meet is ourselves. But I always believe; There will always be a hand who will hold ours sincerely if only we open our hands to behold.
